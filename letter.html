Exotic dance moves

Yes, you are right! As part of my post-doctorate work, I did study exotic tribal dances. My favourite dances are:

Polynesian chicken dance

A little known but very influential dance dating back as far as 300BC, a whole village would dance around in a circle like chickens, to encourage their livestock to be "fruitful".

Icelandic brownian shuffle

Before the modern interpretation of the Brownian motion was defined, the Icelandic people developed a dance where the performers would shake randomly in one spot for hours — an effect that has been known to induce mild nausea!

West African jump style

When the beat drops, you drop too... and jump back up again in time with the bassline. These days however, it's mainly performed by people aged 16–25 in closed circles.
